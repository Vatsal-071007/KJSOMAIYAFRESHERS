FAQ

Questions freshers actually ask

Real answers, no fluff.

Is this run by KJSCE college officially?
Nope — completely unofficial. This is a student-run community, not affiliated with K.J. Somaiya College of Engineering or Somaiya Vidyavihar University in any way. For official information, check kjsce.somaiya.edu.
I'm from a different Somaiya college — can I still join?
Absolutely. The campus is shared — you'll cross paths with students from across Somaiya Vidyavihar University anyway. All are welcome here.
Is accommodation available near campus?
There's on-campus hostel, but spots go fast. Most students find PGs in Vidyavihar or Ghatkopar. Drop the question in the WhatsApp group — someone will point you to a good one.
What clubs should I join in first year?
BloomBox (the E-Cell), Crescendo (cultural fest), IEEE, CSI, robotics, sports committees — KJSCE has a very active extracurricular culture. Recruitments happen in the first semester. Keep an eye on the Discord for announcements.
Who built this and why?
A KJSCE student who wished something like this existed when they were a fresher. If you want to help run it or contribute, hop in the Discord and say hi in #contribute.
